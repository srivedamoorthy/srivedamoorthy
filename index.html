Mahaperiyava initiated many laudable activities to encourage Veda rakshanam and Go rakshanam. Sri Veda Moorthy Chandrashekarendra Saraswati Charitable Trust (SVMCSCT) is a not-for-profit organization registered as public charitable trust and it has reputed professionals as trustees having respected standing in the society. The trust has been formed to encourage and support veda patashalas, Go-shalas aross south India. Our aim is to help them as much as possible by honoring students, adhyapakas and support daily chores. The trust also extends financial support to marriage, educational and medical needs of the poor and needy.
